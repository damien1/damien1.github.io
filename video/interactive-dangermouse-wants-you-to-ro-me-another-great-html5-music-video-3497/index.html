[Interactive] Dangermouse wants you to ro.me - another great HTML5 music video

Tags : Google, HTML5, Music, - Monday, May 30, 2011

Music artist and producer Dangermouse has done it again - or that’s to say Google has done it with HTML5. The new album called Rome is in the style of a (spaghetti western) film soundtrack and if you’ve not hear it — then you’re missing out.

 

HTML5 Music Video wow-ness

 

Google has again helped to create an interactive music video ‘Chrome Experiment’ - to show off some of the awesome-ness that is HTML5.

The track selected is Black feat vocals by Norah Jones. To watch the video you need to open Chrome and go to ro.me

 

Like the music? Then buy Rome (via Amazon).
