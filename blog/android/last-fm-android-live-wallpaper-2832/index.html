Last.fm Android Live Wallpaper

Friday, Apr 8, 2011| Tags: Android, Last.fm, Live Wallpaper, Mobile App Design

I’ve just discovered the (not official) Last.fm live wallpaper for Android. It’s a brilliant change to my boring background on my HTC Desire.

Last.fm is still there tracking my music listening habits – and doing a good job of that. This live wallpaper has a number of options and the free version allows you to create a wallpaper of your favourite artists for example.

The idea for this (app) is also brilliant – it needs no real user interface – and for a small price you get a premium wallpaper with options for weekly charts.

Improvements – rather than charging for the weekly charts – charge for a feature like randomising the wallpaper each day so you look at different artists each day.
