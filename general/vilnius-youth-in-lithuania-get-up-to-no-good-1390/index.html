Vilnius Youth in Lithuania get up to no good

Thursday, Aug 12, 2010| Tags: Lithuania, Vilnius, youth

In Vilnius, the capital city of Lithuania its youth get up to no good. Just like all young people do! Here’s a few photos from the last week to prove how bad these youngsters can be.

Vilnius main drag @4pm on the HOTTEST day (ever) is deserted cause word had got around that 2 skate boarding youth were taking over the town.

Vilnius main tourist attraction is its iconic cathedral and separate bell tower, in this shot the youth of today are street dancing! or is that break dancing?

of course – their mere presence was enough to scare off the toughest of the tourists and ‘pensioners’

At the other end of the square … a much regular bunch of youth who are into acrobatics and stunts (called Parkour) had been scared off by a group of oldies out on a stag do.

that left the skaters to do their worst …
