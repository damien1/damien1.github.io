You can like me on Facebook too

Sunday, May 22, 2011| Tags: Facebook, Social Media

 

Did you know that social media is more than just blogs and RSS feeds?

If you’ve heard of Facebook – you might like to know that I have a fan page – so if you can like my page that would be great.

By liking me — you’ll have daily updates from the latest news and stuff that is published on my website and you’ll be letting me know how much you like Facebook. This will prompt me to work harder to update and improve my fan page.

If … like me … you prefer Twitter to Facebook  — then you can follow me on Twitter

And if you don’t like Twitter either — well stick to reading my blog or subscribe to my RSS Feed.
