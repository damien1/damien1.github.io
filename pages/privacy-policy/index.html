Damien Saunders respects your privacy.

Any personal information you provide to us including and similar to your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address will not be released, sold, or rented to any 3rd party entities or individuals.

 

Credit card details

We will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you do not enter it on any of the forms on White T-shirt Digital New Media.

When you become a customer and purchase a product or service, you could be asked to provide payment method information. This will be entered by you using a payment service provider / payment gateway. These 3rd parties could include Paypal, Google Checkout. Your payment card details will not be entered or stored by me.

We currently use services from Mailchimp and Google.

As a subscriber, customer, client or partner you may email the Account Director for more information on how your details are stored and used.

Linking to 3rd party sites

We are not responsible for the content of external internet sites which we link to. You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.

 

Cookies

A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows White T-shirt Digital New Media to recognize you each time you visit this site (personalisation of content based on your device and if this is your first or repeat visit).

Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and we do not use cookies to collect personal information.

Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as news feeds, web analytics and serving web banner ads. We currently use services from Mailchimp, Google Analytics, Google Adwords and Google Adsense which may set cookies.
