South Africa &#8230; 5 million online and growing

Saturday, Jan 16, 2010 by Damien Saunders Internet, South Africa

South Africa is one of the fastest growing markets for mobile and internet usage is also growing rapidly. A history of poor international bandwidth / connectivity has meant speeds have remained slow. South Africa’s internet users grew from 4.6 million to 5.3 million last year, a rise of 15%, according to research by World Wide Worx, a technology and research and strategy organisation, sponsored by Cisco. The climb follows years of stagnation between 2002 to 2007, when internet penetration in South Africa never rose above 7%.
