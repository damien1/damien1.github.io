London Startup Weekend starts in 2 days (insert panic now)

Wednesday, Jun 2, 2010| Tags: London Startup Weekend

London Startup Weekend, the 54 weekend event for anyone into startups or running their own business starts this Friday. Yes I’m going mental and about to have a breakdown. The weekend is going to awesome.

I’m running around organising food for everyone, organising drinks for everyone and every 2 mins it seems someone wants a new press release or post on the website london.startupweekend.org

My hats off to anyone who steps forward and says ‘Ill organise a 2.5 day event’ by themself!

So next week, something might return to normal … and Ill start to think more about working and getting more interesting content on here.
